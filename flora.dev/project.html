A guide to dressing for your body type!

what is your body type?

If life's a parade, you'd better rock that runway! from character to body structure, everybody's born with a special trait that make them unique. the more eclectic the mix, the better the pegeant. and while people come in different shapes and forms, so do clothes. being comfortable in your own skin is essential to prance around with confidence an poise. to achieve that, you'll need to find outfits that highlight your body's best features. that can be tricky when you don't understand your body type.


The five most common body types

  • Apple (aka oval or round)
  • Pear (aka triangle)
  • Athletic(aka inverted triangle)
  • Rectangle (aka straight or banana)
  • Hourglass
